About Me

I am a creative, adventurous individual that never stops marveling at all there is to see and learn in the world. I pride myself in the fact that first and foremost I am a software engineer, computer scientist, and problem-solver, regardless of languages and technologies utilized. I am always excited to learn new skills required for the job at hand.

My professional interests include full stack development and data visualizations. In my free time, I enjoy powerlifting, mountain biking, skydiving, scuba diving, and international travel. I am also an active vale tudo and MMA fighter.
